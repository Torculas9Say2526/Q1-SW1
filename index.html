My Top 3 Favorite Movies/Series

#1 Alice in Borderland (2020)

Alice in Borderland is an action and thriller series from Japan, the characters in the show play games that are described as "life or death" to return to their real world.

Characters:
-Ryohei Arisu
-Shuntaro Chishiya
-Yuzuha Usagi

#2 The Greatest Showman (2017)

The Greatest Showman is a musical and even described as a thriller movie, it is heavily fictionalized and depicts the life of PT Barnum, a showman and an entertainer.

Characters:
-Anne Wheeler
-Lettie Lutz
-Philip Carlyle

#3 Demon Slayer The Movie: Infinity Castle (2025)

Infinity Castle is an action and adventure movie that is connected to the Japanese anime "Kimetsu no Yaiba" or "Demon Slayer". The characters in this movie fights demon to avenge their loved ones that were killed by those demons.

Characters:
-Tanjiro Kamado
-Zenitsu Agatsuma
-Inosuke Hashibira
